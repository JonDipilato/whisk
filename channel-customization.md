## Channel Customization Fields

Banner image:
- Size: 2048 x 1152 px (6MB or less)
- Design direction: soft sunrise or twilight gradient, floating clouds, tiny lanterns, small forest silhouettes.
- Keep all text in the center safe area so it stays visible on mobile.
- Use only the channel name (no tagline) for long-term clarity.

Profile picture:
- Size: at least 98 x 98 px (4MB or less), PNG or GIF (no animation)
- Design direction: a simple icon like a lantern, firefly, or sleepy fox in warm pastel tones.
- Keep high contrast and avoid tiny details so it reads at small sizes.

Name:
- Use the selected channel name from the shortlist above.

Handle:
- Use the matching handle format above.

Pronouns:
- Optional. Leave blank unless you want them shown.

Language:
- Set to your primary language (example: English).

Channel URL:
- YouTube will assign this after handle selection. Use it consistently across other platforms.

Links:
- Add only real links (website, newsletter, socials) you actively maintain.

## Banner and Profile Concepts (Minimal, Long-Term)

Banner concept A: Lantern Sky (recommended)
- Layout: soft twilight gradient background, light cloud shapes, a thin forest silhouette along the bottom edge.
- Text: channel name centered, single line.
- Palette: #F7E9D5 (cream), #C7D7E4 (mist blue), #8AA2B4 (slate blue), #D7A86E (warm gold).
- Fonts: Title in "Cormorant Garamond Bold" or "Fraunces"; avoid all caps.
- Prompt (if using an image generator): "soft hand-painted twilight sky, pastel gradient, gentle clouds, tiny warm lanterns, thin forest silhouette at bottom, cozy bedtime animation vibe, no text, 16:9, clean and minimal."

Banner concept B: Meadow Dawn (alternate)
- Layout: pale sunrise gradient with a few floating fireflies and a small hill silhouette.
- Text: channel name centered, single line.
- Palette: #FFF3E2 (warm cream), #F3C9A7 (peach), #A7B7C8 (cool blue), #7F8F7A (sage).
- Fonts: Title in "DM Serif Display" or "Playfair Display".
- Prompt: "warm sunrise gradient, soft hills, gentle fireflies, calm pastel palette, hand-painted storybook look, no text, 16:9, minimal."

Profile picture concept A: Lantern Icon (recommended)
- Shape: circular background with a simple lantern icon in the center.
- Colors: background #C7D7E4, lantern #D7A86E, small glow #F7E9D5.

## Content Strategy Snapshot (From Research)

Video specs:
| Metric | Phase 1 | Phase 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Video length | 5 min (300 sec) | 8-10 min (480-600 sec) |
| Scenes | 75 scenes at 4 sec | 120-150 scenes at 4 sec |
| CTR target | 5%+ | 5%+ |
| Retention at 60s | 50%+ | 50%+ |
| Completion | 50%+ | 50%+ |
| Generation time | ~75 min | ~150 min |

Title formulas:
1) "{Character}'s {Theme} Adventure | Bedtime Story"
2) "This {Style} Story Will Help Your Child Sleep"
3) "{Number} {Theme} Stories for Kids | {Style} Animation"

Viral triggers to include:
- Nostalgia: warm, hand-painted, cozy-anime look
- Relatability: gentle family moments
- Calm/ASMR: soft ambient sound and slow pacing
- Surprise: quiet visual reveals

Upload schedule:
| Channel stage | Frequency |
|---|---|
| New (0-1K subs) | 3-5 videos/week |
| Growing (1K-10K) | 2-4 videos/week |
| Established | 1-3 videos/week |
